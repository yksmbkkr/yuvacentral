Vimarsh 2018

Vimarsh is the three-day annual student conclave of YUVA. The conclave has emerged as a powerful avenue for students to get mentorship from experts from respective fields. The conclave is categorised into specific disciplines, wherein students interact with stalwarts to learn about issues and seek mentorship on excelling in the discipline. Hundreds of students across Delhi campuses attend the conclave every year. In the past, reputed institutions including the Neeti Aayog, Finance Ministry, News 18, Bharat Prakashan, Gandhi Darshan Samiti, have partnered and participated in VIMARSH.
