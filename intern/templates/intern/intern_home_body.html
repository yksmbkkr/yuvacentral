Introduction

Youth United for Vision and Action(YUVA) brings you "YUVA Internship Programme". It offers an opportunity to intern and learn the intricacies of different domains spread over Law, Policy Research, Social Work, Science and Technology, Journalism, Commerce, Economics and various other genres at some of the most well established and celebrated institutions across Delhi NCR and also some field studies across the nation. This Scheme seeks to engage students pursuing Under Graduate/Graduate/Post Graduate Degrees or are Research Scholars enrolled in recognized University/Institution within Delhi, as "Interns".
We at YUVA, assure a productive and learning endeavor of yours throughout the period you devote at these internships.
