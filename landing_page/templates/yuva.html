YUVA

Youth United for Vision and Action - A bunch of like minded volunteers united with a motive to provide a common platform to youth for exchanging ideas and polemic discussions.


Who We Are

From the very dawn of any civilization, youth have played a pivotal role in determining the course of the society. The contemporary youth is full of energy, but a large chunk of it goes wasted for lack of Channelization. To catalyse the vitality of the youth and to provide a platform whereby the youth may engage into constructive activism, Youth United for Vision and Action (YUVA) was established in the year 2016. YUVA is a youth based- youth run pedestal upon which the effigy of Academic, Social and Cultural endeavors are carried out. Over the years, YUVA has escalated unto all the major academic institutions pan Delhi-NCR including DU, GGSIPU, JNU, NLU, Ambedkar University, IIT-D, AIIMS,IIMC etc and finds its companionship across more than 1 lakh students, academicians and professionals, committed towards the positive construct of society with the spirit of patriotism. The only membership fee to be a part of YUVA is sincerity, dedication, a positive outlook and a vision to contribute for the society and nation.

Our Initiatives

VIMARSH

VIMARSH is the three-day annual student conclave of YUVA. The conclave has emerged as a powerful avenue for students to get mentorship from experts from respective fields. The conclave is categorised into specific disciplines, wherein students interact with stalwarts to learn about issues and seek mentorship on excelling in the discipline. Hundreds of students across Delhi campuses attend the conclave every year. In the past, reputed institutions including the Neeti Aayog, Finance Ministry, News 18, Bharat Prakashan, Gandhi Darshan Samiti, have partnered and participated in VIMARSH.

YUVA Internship Programme

YUVA has believed that any vision could be formulated and transformed into a constructive action only when the element of knowledge is met with. YUVA teams in academic institutions engage in various academic activities like debating clubs, Moot court societies, study circles, literary clubs, workshops etc.

During last semester breaks, YUVA also launched an internship program whereby the students are facilitated with internships in various forte including Law, Mass Communication, Science and technology, social service, International relations and Marketing & finance. The students are placed at various avenues including research centers across NLU, JNU, VP Chest Hospital, NPL, IARI, Jammu Kashmir Study Center, etc, in Media house like INDIA TV, ZEE NEWS, PANCHJANYA & Organizers etc, In different Industrial sectors like DMRC & RELIANCE YAMUNA POWER LIMITED , various lawyers practicing at the District courts to Hon’ble Supreme Court of India.

Campus Chronicle


Campus Chronicle: An E-magazine of YUVA, Campus Chronicle is one of the leading campus magazines in Delhi with a huge readership. The magazine is entirely run and managed by a large team of student writers and campus correspondents across Delhi campuses. It is an alternative new media which has proved to be a powerful apolitical and democratic mouthpiece of students. The content has been categorised into two broad categories of ‘Campus Stories’ and ‘Academics’.
To keep yourself updated on happenings in Delhi campuses, you can log on to www.campuschronicle.in
Young writers can send their opinion pieces and stories to campuschronicleindia@gmail.com
